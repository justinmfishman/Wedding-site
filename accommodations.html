Where to Stay

Most visitors choose to stay in Waikiki because it has a little bit of everything and there are many different accommodation options. Almost every hotel on the island is in this neighborhood, so we recommend looking for an option that fits your budget, priorities, or loyalty program. We will be staying at Laylow Waikiki, but because almost all Waikiki hotels are in walking distance of each other, we'll all be nearby regardless of your pick.


We will be providing a shuttle service to and from the wedding venue from Waikiki. The shuttle pickup spot will likely be at Aloha Landing Bus Depot at the Sheraton Waikiki Beach Resort, so it might be best to stay within walking distance of that pickup spot.


If you'd like to stay outside Waikiki, we strongly recommend renting a car, and suggest looking for a VRBO in one of these neighborhoods: Kailua (where Jen & Justin live); Lanikai; Hawai'i Kai; Hale'iwa.


Because we are in Hawai'i, we recommend booking as soon as you're able to get the best rates.
