Beach Day

Welcome to O'ahu!

Jen & Justin will be under a pop-up beach tent at Kailua Beach from 10am–1pm.

Feel free to stop by and say hello! Come and go as you please.

We are excited to share our favorite beach with you all, just a block from our home.

The water is warm and the waves are gentle, so join us for a swim.

The beach park has public restrooms and outdoor showers.

What to Bring

Swimsuits, towels, umbrellas, beach chairs. Kailua Beach Adventures (one block away) rents kayaks and paddle boards — paddle to the bird sanctuary or the Mokulua Islands.

Food & Drink Nearby

Kalapawai Market

Sandwiches, pizza, lunch bowls, snacks.

Island Snow

Our favorite shave ice (rumored to be Obama's too). Try the all-natural flavors!

Bob's Pizzeria

Pizza and calzones.

The Sunrise Shack

Acai bowls, smoothies, coffee.

Buzz's

Iconic restaurant (opened 1962), steaks, local seafood, cocktails — just across the street from the beach.
