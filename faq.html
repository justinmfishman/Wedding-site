Q & A

For all our friends and family who have lots of questions, please check out our Q & A first!

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by June 15th, so we can have an accurate headcount. :)

Can I bring a date?

Please check your invite for your +1 or ask one of us!

What should I wear?

Island Formal / Aloha Formal attire.

Women: Wear what makes you feel comfortable and confident! Leave heels at home due to terrain.

Men: Slacks, dress shoes, and either a dress shirt or a nice aloha shirt. Aloha shirts are considered formal wear here. Recommended brands: Reyn Spooner, Kahala, Sig Zane, or Tori Richards. Check Nordstrom Rack in Waikiki for deals.

August in Hawai'i is hot! Wear breathable clothes, feel free to bring a hat.

What is Shabbat and what is the Shabbat event?

Shabbat is a Jewish tradition of a Friday night dinner with family. We're hosting a casual picnic at Ala Moana Beach Park. All faiths welcome! No need to fly in early just for this.

Where is the Beach Day and what should I bring?

Kailua Beach, where Jen and Justin live. More info under the Beach Day section.

How do I get to the ceremony & reception?

The ceremony is at Kualoa Ranch. We'll have shuttle buses from Waikiki. Parking is also available at the venue for those who drive.
