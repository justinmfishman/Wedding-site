Friday, August 28, 2026

Shabbat Dinner

🕐 5:30 PM 👔 Casual
📍 Picnic Area 40, Ala Moana Boulevard, Honolulu, HI

Shabbat dinner is a Jewish traditional meal with family and friends on Friday night. Everyone is welcome to attend! Please don't fly in early just to make the dinner; the idea is to provide a welcoming meal to anyone who happens to be on the island on Friday night. We understand that many of our guests won't be able to get here in time for the meal. Dinner is a casual picnic at Ala Moana Beach Park, near Waikiki. Dress comfortably. We'll have food, tents, and chairs, so just bring yourselves.
