Travel

Getting In

Please fly into Daniel K. Inouye International Airport (HNL).

You can easily catch an Uber/Lyft to your hotel or rent a car at the airport. Car rentals are also available through turo.com.

Getting Around

Your choice whether to rent a car or not. If you stay in Waikiki, there are beaches and things to do within walking distance. Some bookable activities will shuttle you back and forth from Waikiki as well.

That being said, if you have time during your trip, we recommend renting a car and driving around the island to see the best parts, away from the hustle and bustle of Waikiki.

We will have a shuttle from Waikiki to the wedding venue at Kualoa Ranch on the day of the ceremony.
